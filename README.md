# Pulse-diagnpsis

Pulse Levels
0 - Absent 
1 + Low
2 - Normal 
3 - Excess

# Mistakes Influence Organs
Mistakes lead to an organ excess conditions based on our daily activities.

# Spleen Excess
- Eating food during 9 to to 11 AM
- Eating sweets

# Stomach Excess 
- Drinking water while eating food
- Drinking water immediately after eating food
- Eating liquid food like butter milk, moore sadham, palaya sadham

# Heart Excess
- Eating Ice Cream or Cold items between 11 - 1 PM

# Heart Deficient
- Liftting heavy weight like bucket, bags eventhough not able to lift between 11 - 1 PM

# Small intestine Deficient
- Eating food between 1-3 PM
 
# Urinary Bladder Deficient
- Not drinking water or Not urinating between 3-5 PM

# Urinary Bladder Deficient
- Drinking water fastly or at one shot between 3 - 5 PM

# Kidney Excess
- Drinking cool drinks or cold beverages or eating cold items between 5 - 7 PM
- Staying nearby lake, house surrounded by water
- Living in a room with wet walls or below water tank
- Drainage water flowing under Living room
- Any other water reserves surrounded by on the living area
- Eating Ice Cream between 5 - 7 PM
- If K & LV = 3:
  - Chocolate Flavor Ice Cream
- If K & LU - 3:
  - Venilla Flavor Ice Cream

# Liver Excess
- Vatha kuzhambu, Kaara Kuzhambu
- Lemon rice, puliyodharai, Puli sertha kulambu or sadham
- Pickle, More sadham
if K & Lv - 3:
  - Puzhicha thairu (Heavily Fermented curd), more kuzhambu
- Mobile used between 1-3 AM
- if Lu & Lv - 3:
  - Sleeplessness with Rib pain
  - Purandu Purandu thoonguvaanga
- If Lv, Lu & LI - 3:
  - Gastric with improper sleep

# Gall Bladder Excess
- Drinking Water
- Not Sleeping between 11 - 1 AM

# Lung Excess
- Walking in Cold Weather
- Drinking Chill Water

# Large Intestine Excess
- Eating Food in night time or Triple Warmer Time

# Percardium Excess
- Drinking water after food or Eating Liquid Food
# Pericardium Deficient
- Eating food in Liver time
- Eating food after 9 PM will also lead to acid reflex

# Triple Warmer Excess
- Eating food between 9 - 11 PM
